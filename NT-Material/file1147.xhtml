Ephesians
Chapter 1

Salutation

1:1 From Paul,1 an apostle of Christ Jesus by the will of God, to the saints [in Ephesus],2 the faithful3 in Christ Jesus. 1:2 Grace and peace to you4 from God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ!

Spiritual Blessings in Christ

1:3 Blessed5 is6 the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who has blessed7 us with every spiritual blessing in the heavenly realms in Christ. 1:4 For8 he chose us in Christ9 before the foundation of the world that we should be holy and blameless10 before him11 in love.12 1:5 He did this by predestining13 us to adoption as his14 legal heirs15 through Jesus Christ, according to the pleasure16 of his will— 1:6 to the praise of the glory of his grace17 that he has freely bestowed on us in his dearly loved Son.18 1:7 In him19 we have redemption through his blood,20 the forgiveness of our offenses,21 according to the riches of his grace 1:8 that he lavished on us in all wisdom and insight. 1:9 He did this when he revealed22 to us the mystery23 of his will, according to his good pleasure that he set forth24 in Christ,25 1:10 toward the administration of the fullness of the times, to head up26 all things in Christ—the things in heaven27 and the things on earth.28 1:11 In Christ29 we too have been claimed as God’s own possession,30 since we were predestined according to the purpose of him who accomplishes all things according to the counsel of his will 1:12 so that we, who were the first to set our hope31 on Christ,32 would be to the praise of his glory. 1:13 And when33 you heard the word of truth (the gospel of your salvation)—when you believed in Christ34 —you were marked with the seal35 of the promised Holy Spirit,36 1:14 who is the down payment37 of our inheritance, until the redemption of God’s own possession,38 to the praise of his glory.

Prayer for Wisdom and Revelation

1:15 For this reason,39 because I40 have heard41 of your faith in the Lord Jesus and your love42 for all the saints, 1:16 I do not cease to give thanks for you when I remember you43 in my prayers. 1:17 I pray that44 the God of our Lord Jesus Christ, the glorious Father,45 will give you spiritual wisdom and revelation46 in your growing knowledge of him,47 1:18 —since the eyes of your48 heart have been enlightened49 —so that you can know what is the hope of his calling,50 what is the wealth of his glorious51 inheritance in the saints, 1:19 and what is the incomparable52 greatness of his power toward53 us who believe, as displayed in54 the exercise of his immense strength.55 1:20 This power56 he exercised57 in Christ when he raised him58 from the dead and seated him59 at his right hand in the heavenly realms60 1:21 far above every rule and authority and power and dominion and every name that is named, not only in this age but also in the one to come. 1:22 And God61 put62 all things under Christ’s63 feet,64 and gave him to the church as head over all things.65 1:23 Now the church is66 his body, the fullness of him who fills all in all.67
