Ephesians
Chapter 5

Live in Love

5:1 Therefore, be1 imitators of God as dearly loved children 5:2 and live2 in love, just as Christ also loved us3 and gave himself for us, a sacrificial and fragrant offering4 to God. 5:3 But5 among you there must not be either sexual immorality, impurity of any kind,6 or greed, as these are not fitting for the saints.7 5:4 Neither should there be vulgar speech, foolish talk, or coarse jesting—all of which are out of character—but rather thanksgiving. 5:5 For you can be confident of this one thing:8 that no person who is immoral, impure, or greedy (such a person is an idolater) has any inheritance in the kingdom of Christ and God.

Live in the Light

5:6 Let nobody deceive you with empty words, for because of these things God’s wrath comes on the sons of disobedience.9 5:7 Therefore do not be sharers with them,10 5:8 for you were at one time darkness, but now you are11 light in the Lord. Live12 like children of light— 5:9 for the fruit of the light13 consists in14 all goodness, righteousness, and truth— 5:10 trying to learn15 what is pleasing to the Lord. 5:11 Do not participate in the unfruitful deeds of darkness, but rather16 expose them.17 5:12 For the things they do18 in secret are shameful even to mention. 5:13 But all things being exposed by the light are made visible. 5:14 For everything made visible is light, and for this reason it says:19

“Awake,20 O sleeper!21

Rise from the dead,

and Christ will shine on you!”22

Live Wisely

5:15 Therefore consider carefully how you live—not as unwise but as wise, 5:16 taking advantage of every opportunity, because the days are evil. 5:17 For this reason do not be foolish, but be wise23 by understanding24 what the Lord’s will is. 5:18 And do not get drunk with wine, which25 is debauchery,26 but be filled by the Spirit,27 5:19 speaking to one another in psalms, hymns, and spiritual songs, singing and making music28 in29 your hearts to the Lord, 5:20 always giving thanks to God the Father for all things30 in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ, 5:21 and submitting to one another out of reverence for Christ.31

Exhortations to Households

5:22 32 Wives, submit33 to your husbands as to the Lord, 5:23 because the husband is the head of the wife as also Christ is the head of the church (he himself being the savior of the body). 5:24 But as the church submits to Christ, so also wives should submit to their husbands in everything. 5:25 Husbands, love your34 wives just as Christ loved the church and gave himself for her 5:26 to sanctify her by cleansing her35 with the washing of the water by the word, 5:27 so that he36 may present the church to himself as glorious—not having a stain or wrinkle, or any such blemish, but holy and blameless.37 5:28 In the same way38 husbands ought to love their wives as their own bodies. He who loves his wife loves himself. 5:29 For no one has ever hated his own body,39 but he feeds it and takes care of it, just as Christ also does the church, 5:30 because we are members of his body.40 5:31 For this reason a man will leave his father and mother and will be joined to his wife, and the two will become41 one flesh.42 5:32 This mystery is great—but I am actually43 speaking with reference to Christ and the church. 5:33 Nevertheless,44 each one of you must also love his own wife as he loves himself,45 and the wife must46 respect47 her husband.
